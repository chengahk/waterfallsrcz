About Our Church

Rooted in Christ. Growing together in faith, love and service.

Who We Are

The Reformed Church in Zimbabwe – Waterfalls Congregation is a vibrant Christian community based in Harare. We are committed to worshipping God faithfully and serving our community.

Our Vision

To build a Christ-centered community that transforms lives through faith, love and service.

Our Mission

• Preach the Word of God faithfully
• Nurture believers spiritually
• Serve the community
• Raise a generation rooted in Christ

Our Beliefs

We believe in the authority of the Bible, salvation through Jesus Christ, and living a life that reflects the love of God.
