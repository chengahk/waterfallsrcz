WELCOME TO OUR CHURCH

Welcome to the Reformed Church in Zimbabwe – Waterfalls Congregation. We are a Christ-centered family committed to nurturing faith, building strong relationships and transforming lives through the Word of God and the power of community.

Who We Are

We are a Christ-centered congregation committed to faith, fellowship, and spiritual growth. Our calling is to build lives, strengthen families, and impact our community through the love of Christ.

Our Mission

To nurture a Christ-centered community that grows in faith, strengthens fellowship, and serves others with love.

Our Vision

To be a spiritually vibrant congregation that transforms lives and raises disciples committed to God's Kingdom.

Our Core Values

Christ-Centered Living, Faith & Prayer, Love & Unity, Service, Discipleship and Integrity.

Our Ministries

Men's Fellowship

Equipping men with faith, courage and wisdom to serve God faithfully.

Women's Fellowship

Uniting women in prayer, purpose and strength through God's unfailing love.

Boys' Fellowship

Strengthening character, guiding boys to grow as faithful men of God.

Girls' Fellowship

Nurturing faith, building confidence, inspiring girls to walk with God.

Youth Ministry

Empowering you hearts to lead, serve, and shine boldly in Christ.

Sunday School Ministry

Teaching truth, shaping lives, planting seeds of faith for tomorrow' s generation.
